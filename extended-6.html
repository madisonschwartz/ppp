Prayer Plant

Maranta leuconeura

Price: $23.22 (Home Depot)

Growing Conditions:

  • Medium to bright indirect light
  • Prefers a higher humidity
  • Fertilize year-round with balanced fertilizer, but less frequently during the winter months
  • Use filtered water every 1-2 weeks when soil is dry

Fact: The “prayer” in the plant’s name comes from its tendency to droop at night and when they need water!
