Parlor Palm

Chamaedorea elegans

Price: $16.99 (Home Depot)

Growing Conditions:

  • Bright indirect/filtered light
  • Above average humidity and temperature
  • Use a balanced fertilizer monthly
  • Water when soil is dry (be careful of overwatering)

Fact: The parlor palm is the slowest-growing indoor palm! Don't be alarmed if it isn't changing as much as the other plants in your collection.
