Calathea Orbifolia

Goeppertia orbifolia

Price: $9.75 (Etsy)

Growing Conditions:

  • Bright indirect light
  • Prefers higher humidity and temperatures
  • Fertilize monthly during the growing season
  • Water when the top of the soil is dry

Fact: Just like prayer plants, calathea leaves droop at night! They "awaken" again when exposed to light from the sun.
