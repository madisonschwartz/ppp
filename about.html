What is Plant Joy?

Welcome to Plant Joy©, your ultimate guide regarding houseplants and how to care for them. Our mission is simple: to foster a community of plant enthusiasts (both seasoned green thumbs and aspiring plant parents alike) by providing access to comprehensive and attainable resources that will inspire you to be the best you can be along your journey. We believe that houseplants have the capacity to bring beauty and fulfillment into one’s life, and we’re here to help make that happen.
